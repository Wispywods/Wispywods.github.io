I don't like most F2P games

To me, a game being free to play is usually something that actually discourages me from playing

Some reasons I don't like F2P games is because of how they're usually designed, while others are more abstract feelings they give

F2P Game Design I don't like

F2P games often interrupt game flow by forcing the player to wait to do something or pay to bypass the cooldown.

Besides that, a lot of F2P games don't really end. Even the story-based ones usually go on far too long. Having to wait for the story to be finished can really kill the hype.

Personal problems with F2P games

This is more abstract and harder to explain, but I feel the very fact that they're free to play can take away from the excitement of getting them.

When you want a paid game, but have to think about it first, the time you spend thinking builds up tension, which is released when you play the game if you buy it.

F2P games don't require as much thought to download, you can get them on a whim, so that tension and excitement is never built.
